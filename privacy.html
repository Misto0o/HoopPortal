2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Create an account: Email address, password, full name, phone number, location (city/state)
  • Complete your profile: Profile picture, athletic position, height, weight, school name, class year, game description, coach preferences
  • Add contact information: Player email, player phone, parent/guardian names and contact information
  • Upload content: Highlight reels, video links (YouTube/Vimeo)
  • Make payments: Payment information processed through Stripe (we do not store full credit card details)
  • Communicate with us: Feedback, support requests, or inquiries

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you access our Site, we automatically collect certain information:

  • Usage Information: Pages visited, time spent on pages, searches performed, profiles viewed
  • Service Worker Data: Cache and offline functionality tracking (not personal data)

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Google OAuth: When you sign up with Google, we receive your name, email, and profile picture from Google
  • Stripe: Payment processor provides transaction confirmation and status
  • Supabase: Authentication and database services

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

3.1 Primary Uses

  • Account Management: Create and maintain your account, verify identity, authenticate login
  • Service Delivery: Provide the HoopPortal platform and features (profile visibility, searching, liking, messaging capabilities)
  • Visibility Control: Show your profile to coaches (only if subscription is active)
  • Payment Processing: Process subscriptions and payments through Stripe
  • Highlight Reels: Store and display your athletic highlight videos

3.2 Secondary Uses

  • Improve and optimize our Site and services
  • Send service-related announcements and updates
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and fraud
  • Monitor and analyze usage trends and Site performance
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Information We DO Share

  • With Coaches: Your profile is visible to coaches ONLY if your subscription is active. Visible information includes: name, position, height, weight, class year, school, location, profile picture, game description, and season stats
  • Contact Information: Your contact details are visible only to users you've connected with or accepted
  • Stripe: Payment information (processed securely; we don't store card numbers)
  • Supabase: User data for authentication, storage, and database management

4.2 Information We DO NOT Share

  • We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties
  • We do NOT share your data with advertisers or marketing companies
  • We do NOT share your password with anyone
  • We do NOT share your payment information beyond Stripe

4.3 Legal Disclosures

We may disclose your information when required by law or when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to:

  • Comply with legal obligations, court orders, or government requests
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and other agreements
  • Protect the safety, rights, and property of HoopPortal, users, or the public
  • Prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing in connection with the Site

5. Data Security

HoopPortal takes the security of your personal information seriously. We implement comprehensive security measures:

5.1 Security Measures

  • Supabase Security: Industry-standard encryption for data at rest and in transit (SSL/TLS)
  • Stripe Security: PCI-DSS Level 1 compliance for payment processing; we never store full credit card information
  • Authentication: Secure password hashing and session management
  • Service Worker: Offline caching with automatic updates to prevent outdated code
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
Important: While we implement strong security measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your password.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Account Deletion: When you delete your account, ALL personal data is removed immediately from all database tables. No backup copies are retained.
  • Data Tables We Maintain: User data is stored in the following tables while your account is active:
    • coach_profiles - Coaching information and details
    • user_profiles - Account information (email, name, subscription status)
    • player_profiles - Athletic profile data (position, height, weight, school, stats)
    • player_stats - Season statistics and performance metrics
    • parent_guardians - Parent/guardian contact information
    • player_contact - Player contact details
    • highlight_reels - Video links and uploaded highlight content
    • liked_players - Players you've liked/saved
    • player_views - View count tracking for analytics
  • Log Data: Server logs are retained for 90 days for security purposes only
  • Backup Systems: Supabase maintains automatic backups; deleted data is purged from backups within 30 days

Important: When you request account deletion, your data is permanently deleted immediately and cannot be recovered. This action is irreversible.
