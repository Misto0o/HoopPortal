Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately.

How do I add highlight reels?

After selecting a plan and creating your profile, you'll have access to upload highlights. Basic allows 2, Premium allows 5.

Is my profile searchable right away?

Yes! Once you activate a plan, your profile becomes searchable and coaches can find you.

What's the difference between Basic and Premium?

Premium gets you featured at the top of coach searches with a premium badge, plus 3 extra highlight reels.
